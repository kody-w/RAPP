Reading the map

  • Hub — repos with many edges (e.g. RAPP, rapp-installer, RAR) sit centrally.
  • Cluster — same color = same category. Tight clusters mean a coherent sub-ecosystem.
  • Drifter — isolated nodes have no detected dependency edges; they may be experiments, archived, or genuinely standalone.
  • Lineage edge — derived from naming (X-private → X, X-archive → X, X-canary → X).
