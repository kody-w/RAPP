Three-minute setup. One agent does the work.

📱 mobile-friendly

1 Get the Discord webhook URL

In your Discord app on mobile:

  1. Open the channel where the neighborhood will live
  2. Tap the channel name at the top → Edit Channel
  3. Scroll to Integrations → Webhooks → New Webhook
  4. Tap Copy Webhook URL

No webhook permission? Ask the server admin to enable it for your channel.
