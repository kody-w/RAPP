## Tips for Great Gameplay

### Roleplaying

âœ… **Describe actions in character:**
- "I draw my sword and challenge the orc to single combat"
- "I bow respectfully to the elven queen"
- "I slam my fist on the table and demand answers"

âŒ **Don't just state mechanics:**
- "I attack"
- "Use persuasion"
- "Roll perception"

### Strategy

**Combat:**
- Use cover and terrain
- Focus fire on dangerous enemies
- Conserve spell slots for tough fights
- Don't be afraid to retreat!

**Exploration:**
- Search thoroughly
- Talk to everyone
- Take notes on clues
- Map dungeons mentally

**Social:**
- Pay attention to NPC personalities
- Use appropriate skills (Persuasion vs Intimidation)
- Build relationships
- Information is valuable!

### Resource Management

**Spell Slots:**
- Don't blow everything in first combat
- Save high-level slots for bosses
- Cantrips are free!

**HP:**
- Short rest when possible
- Use healing potions strategically
- Long rest when safe

**Gold:**
- Buy better equipment
- Stock up on potions
- Save for important purchases

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I play multiple characters?**
A: Currently one character per game, but you can save multiple games!

**Q: What happens if I die?**
A: You make death saving throws. If you fail, your adventure ends... but you can create a new character!

**Q: Can I customize my character more?**
A: The DM will work with you! Just describe what you want.

**Q: How does magic work?**
A: Spell slots represent your magical energy. You recover them on long rests.

**Q: Can I switch campaigns?**
A: Yes! Save your current game and start a new one anytime.

**Q: How long do campaigns last?**
A: As long as you want! Could be one session or many.

**Q: Can I create my own campaign?**
A: Absolutely! Use campaign_type='custom' and describe what you want.
